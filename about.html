About World Clock

World Clock is a simple and accurate tool to compare time zones and daylight saving time (DST) around the world. You can add multiple clocks showing analog and digital times for different cities, making it easy to plan meetings or calls across time zones.

This tool uses your browser's built-in internationalization API to provide up-to-date and reliable time information without any need for external servers.

Our goal is to provide a free, easy-to-use world clock to help everyone coordinate time globally.
